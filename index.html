Menu

Bar Menu

We are proud to introduce our collection of crafted beers. Try our special recipes of beers and ciders separately or in a beer flight. If you’re not much into beer, order some wine, a spirit, or a cocktail. We also have a broad selection of soft drinks.

Food Menu

We adjust our menu to any tastes and needs. Apart from an impressive grill menu, we have vegetarian and gluten-free options. Try a single meal or share a plate with the company. Also, ask a waiter about the best gastronomic match to your drink.

Happy Hour Offers

This is a special offer available from 3 to 5 PM. Check the Happy Hour menu online or ask a waiter about the special offer, so you can save your money and enjoy a delicious meal. Hours may change from time to time, so make sure to subscribe to our updates.

Our Story

Who we are and how we make drinks

About Beer Boutique

We strive to promote the culture of drinking beer as something beyond a football match. It is a drink with a long history and an immense depth of flavor, which is why we build our menu foods around their compatibility with the beers. Beer Boutique is a place to find the favorite beer flavor and learn more about the drink.

More about us

Process of creating

Our selection of beers is a result of long and scrupulous experiments that give us thousands of taste combinations. Each and every beer of ours has a unique recipe and a solid standard of preparation, supervised closely by our beer masters. We want you to feel all the richness of an original draft beer.

Amber Stout

It is a rich and refined taste for true stout lovers. With 7-8% of alcohol and a strong bitter flavor, Amber Stout is not a beer for a drinking competition.

Barely Wine

Barley Wine is a style of dark ales. It is one of the strongest ales in our collection. Barley Wine has a dark color and dense texture. It is a wonderful beer for a fall or winter.

Herr Weizenbier

It is our most popular wheat beer. It is deeply pale, a little sour, and goes perfectly on a hot summer day. Herr Weizenbier is our tribute to the Bavarian brewery tradition.
