About Beer Boutique

We strive to promote the culture of drinking beer as something beyond a football match. It is a drink with a long history and an immense depth of flavor, which is why we build our menu foods around their compatibility with the beers. Beer Boutique is a place to find the favorite beer flavor and learn more about the drink.

Process of creating

Our selection of beers is a result of long and scrupulous experiments that give us thousands of taste combinations. Each and every beer of ours has a unique recipe and a solid standard of preparation, supervised closely by our beer masters. We want you to feel all the richness of an original draft beer.
